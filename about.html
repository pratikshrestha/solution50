What We Do

The Solution50 Ecosystem

Two distinct divisions. One unified mission.

Division One

Biometric Innovation

Our fingerprinting division is dedicated to precision. By bridging the gap between traditional ink-and-roll methods and modern digital requirements (EFT), we empower agencies and individuals to meet FBI and federal standards with zero friction.

We believe that identity verification should be a seamless gateway, not a bureaucratic hurdle. Every system we deploy is engineered for accuracy, compliance, and operational simplicity.
